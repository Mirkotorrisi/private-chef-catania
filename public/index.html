    About

    Born in Catania, between the slopes of Etna and the coasts of eastern Sicily, the place of origin of multiple culinary traditions inspired by the union between land and sea. My passion was born in the kitchen at home, during the preparation of Sunday family lunches, using ingredients from all over Sicily. Those same aromas, pleasing to the nose and palate, continue to be a source of inspiration, both to rediscover the ancient flavors and to reinvent myself in new dishes. I was lucky enough to be able to expand my knowledge even outside my country, among the best French and English cuisines. Knowing the beloved native flavors and those of the rest of the world that intrigued me, I was able to invent menus ready to satisfy any palate. My goal is to satisfy these palates, in the intimacy of home, giving rise to the concept of in home chef for you!
